# Sales & Customer Dashboards (Tableau)

This project contains two interactive Tableau dashboards designed to help stakeholders analyze **sales performance** and **customer behavior**.  
It includes year-over-year KPIs, trend analysis, product insights, customer segmentation, and interactive filtering.

---

## Dashboards Overview

### **1. Sales Performance Dashboard**
Provides a complete view of key sales metrics and YoY changes.

#### **Key Features**
- **KPI Overview:** Sales, profit, and quantity (current year vs previous year)
- **Monthly Trends:** YoY comparison across KPIs
- **Best & Worst Months:** Clearly highlighted
- **Product Subcategory Comparison:** Sales & profit side-by-side
- **Weekly Trends:** Weekly sales & profit with average lines and highlights
- **Interactive Filtering:** Category, subcategory, region, state, city

---

### **2. Customer Dashboard**
Helps understand customer behavior, loyalty, and profitability.

#### **Key Features**
- **Customer KPIs:** Total customers, sales per customer, total orders (YoY comparison)
- **Customer Trends:** Monthly changes in customer metrics
- **Customer Distribution:** Based on number of orders
- **Top 10 Customers by Profit:** Rank, orders, sales, profit, and last order date
- **Interactive visual exploration tools**

---

## Dashboard Interactivity

- **Dynamic Year Selector** to view historical data  
- **Dashboard navigation buttons**  
- **Cross-chart filtering**  
- **Flexible filters** for product & location information
